Top movies to watch

My top three movies

Red, White & Royal Blue

Alex Claremont-Diaz, the first son of the United States, and young Prince Henry fall in love. However, considering their high-profile public lives, they must keep their relationship a secret at all costs.

The Tourist

Frank, an American tourist, is visiting Italy to deal with a recent heartbreak. Elise sits next to him on a train going to Venice and convinces him that he is her lover who is wanted by the police.

Cry-Baby

A bad boy manages to attract and win over the girl he likes. However, when her boyfriend learns about it, he sets out to create problems for him.
